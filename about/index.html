Somos San Diego Mesa Community College.

Here at Mesa, it is our mission to empower our diverse student body so that they may reach their educational goals and shape the future. Perched on top of the Clairemont Mesa and adjacent to Tecolote Canyon Natural Park, we are one of the largest community colleges in California, and the largest in the City of San Diego with a student population of approximately 30,000. We are committed to being the Leading College of Equity and Excellence and are proud to be formally designated a Hispanic-Serving Institution by the United States Department of Education. Being an HSI means we are a reflection of our exceptional students, their cultures, values, and life experiences. With Latinx, Chicanx, and Hispanic students making up over 38% of the student body, we strive each and every day to promote student success at every level to support them in reaching their educational goals.
